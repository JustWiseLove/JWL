Theme: Galatians 6:10; 1 John 3:17

- Compassion requires noticing and acting on others’ needs.
- Imitate by listening, preaching, praying for others, and supporting relief efforts.
Scriptures: John 2:1-11, 1 John 3:17, Galatians 6:10.

Theme: Matthew 6:2-4; 1 Peter 5:5
Key Points:
- Jesus performed the Cana miracle discreetly (John 2:9-11), seeking Jehovah’s approval.

Key Points:
- Jesus provided abundant wine at Cana (John 2:6-8), reflecting Jehovah’s generosity.

2:20 How “the Lamb of God” Takes Away Sin

Theme: John 1:29; John 3:14-16
Key Points:
- Sin, inherited from Adam, pulls humanity down (Romans 3:23, 5:12), but Jesus’ sacrifice offers redemption (Romans 7:19-25).
- As the “Lamb of God” (John 1:29), Jesus provides permanent atonement, unlike animal sacrifices.
- His sacrifice, like the copper serpent, requires faith for everlasting life (John 3:14-16, 1 John 4:10).
- Benefits include forgiveness, a clean conscience, and resurrection hope (Romans 6:23, Acts 24:15).
- Imitate by accepting forgiveness and sharing the ransom’s hope (Psalm 32:1-2).
Scriptures: Romans 3:23, 5:12, 7:19-20, 24-25, John 1:29, John 3:14-16, 1 John 4:10, Psalm 32:1-2, Romans 6:23, Acts 24:15.
Remarkable Because: Jesus’ sacrifice permanently removes sin’s penalty, offering hope to all who exercise faith.
