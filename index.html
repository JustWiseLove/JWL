Buy Truth and Never Sell It

Truth Is Priceless

Proverbs 23:23 urges us to "buy truth and never sell it." Truth from Jehovah is a treasure worth more than gold. It’s not a casual suggestion—it’s a command to seek what lasts. This truth isn’t found in human ideas or shifting opinions. It’s hidden in God’s Word, waiting for those who dig deep. Crave real answers? Hunt for it now, because it unlocks everlasting life.

“Buy truth and do not sell it—wisdom and discipline and understanding.” —Proverbs 23:23

The Source of Truth

Jehovah is the God of truth. His Word, the Bible, is where we find it. Jesus said, “Your word is truth” (John 17:17). Unlike the world’s lies that fade, Jehovah’s truth endures forever. It’s not cheap—it costs effort, time, and humility to uncover. But once you grasp it, no price is too high. Search for it like hidden treasure, because it leads to life.

“Sanctify them by means of the truth; your word is truth.” —John 17:17

Digging for Treasure

Finding truth takes work. Proverbs 2:4-5 says to seek it “as for hidden treasures.” That means studying the Bible, not just skimming it. Jehovah reveals his purpose—his Kingdom, the end of suffering, a paradise earth—to those who search. The world offers distractions, but they’re worthless compared to this. Dig in now; the reward is knowing Jehovah and his Son.

“If you search for it as for silver, and you seek it as for hidden treasures, then you will understand the fear of Jehovah, and you will find the knowledge of God.” —Proverbs 2:4-5

Never Sell It

Once you have Jehovah’s truth, hold it tight. The world tempts us to trade it for money, approval, or ease. But Jesus warned, “What will a man give in exchange for his life?” (Matthew 16:26). Truth is your lifeline to God’s promises—don’t let it go. It’s the foundation for living with purpose now and forever.

“For what will it benefit a man if he gains the whole world but loses his own life? Or what will a man give in exchange for his life?” —Matthew 16:26

The Reward of Truth

Truth isn’t just knowledge—it’s power. It frees you from lies and fear. It shows the way to everlasting life. Jesus said, “This means everlasting life, their taking in knowledge of you, the only true God, and of the one whom you sent, Jesus Christ” (John 17:3). Buy it now. Study. Pray. Act. Jehovah’s truth is the treasure that never fails.

“This means everlasting life, their taking in knowledge of you, the only true God, and of the one whom you sent, Jesus Christ.” —John 17:3
