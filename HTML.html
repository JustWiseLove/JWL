THE BIBLE

2. The Bible was penned by over 40 human writers.—2 Peter 1:21; 2 Timothy 3:16; Isaiah 40:8.

The Bible was penned by over 40 human writers—kings, prophets, fishermen—spanning 1,600 years, yet its unity stems from Jehovah’s holy spirit. Unlike pagan texts filled with contradictory myths, its consistent message across centuries proves divine authorship. Historically, its composition began with Moses around 1513 B.C.E. and concluded with John’s writings around 98 C.E., surviving persecution and translation into thousands of languages. Science supports its accuracy, such as the hydrologic cycle in Job 36:27-28, predating modern understanding. This refutes false worship’s claim of human origin, affirming Jehovah as its true Author.

Unified over 1,600 years.

"Men spoke from God as they were moved by holy spirit." - 2 Peter 1:21

"All Scripture is inspired of God." - 2 Timothy 3:16

"The word of our God endures forever." - Isaiah 40:8

THE BIBLE

3. The Bible’s scientific accuracy shines through.—Job 26:7; Isaiah 40:22; Job 36:27-28.

The Bible’s scientific accuracy shines through statements made long before human discovery, such as the earth’s suspension in space, a fact unknown in ancient times. Unlike false worship’s flat-earth myths or Babylonian cosmologies, it hints at its spherical shape. Historically, its sanitation laws predate germ theory, saving lives. These facts dismantle claims of inaccuracy, proving the Bible’s divine origin and its superiority over pagan falsehoods.

Earth’s shape and suspension.

"He suspends the earth upon nothing." - Job 26:7

"He dwells above the circle of the earth." - Isaiah 40:22

"He draws up the drops of water; they filter as rain." - Job 36:27-28

GOD

4. Jehovah, God’s personal name, appears over 7,000 times.—Psalm 83:18; Exodus 3:15; Isaiah 42:8.

Jehovah, God’s personal name, appears over 7,000 times in the original texts. False worship obscures this name with titles like 'Lord,' a practice rooted in superstition and tradition, not Scripture. Historically, the Tetragrammaton (YHWH) was revered by Israel, yet removed by later scribes influenced by pagan customs. Science offers no contradiction, but the Bible’s consistent use across millennia confirms its authenticity. Knowing Jehovah’s name sets true worship apart from the nameless deities of false religion.

YHWH, often removed.

"You, whose name is Jehovah, are the Most High." - Psalm 83:18

"Jehovah... this is my name forever." - Exodus 3:15

"I am Jehovah. That is my name." - Isaiah 42:8

He’s the ransom and future King, refuting false claims of co-equality and affirming Jehovah’s supremacy in true worship.
