UNSHAKABLE

“Therefore, since we are receiving a Kingdom that cannot be shaken, let us continue to have undeserved kindness, through which we may acceptably offer God sacred service with godly fear and awe.” — Hebrews 12:28

Jehovah’s promises and His unshakable Kingdom stand firm, anchored by fulfilled prophecies, divine power, and the enduring truth of His Word.
