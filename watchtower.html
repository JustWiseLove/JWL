Study Article 1

Scripture: “Give to Jehovah the glory belonging to his name.” — Psalm 96:8

FOCUS: We will see how we can glorify Jehovah and why we must avoid doing anything that would bring reproach on his name.

1. What does it mean to glorify Jehovah?

To glorify Jehovah means to reflect his qualities in our speech and conduct, giving honor to his holy name. As Jesus said in John 17:4: “I have glorified you on the earth, having finished the work you gave me to do.” Like Jesus, we honor Jehovah by obeying His commands and promoting His name.

2. How did Jesus give glory to his Father?

Jesus glorified Jehovah by making His name known and reflecting His qualities perfectly. He stated: “I have made your name manifest to the men you gave me out of the world.” — John 17:6.

3. How can we imitate Jesus in giving glory to Jehovah?

We imitate Jesus by actively participating in the ministry, obeying Jehovah’s commands, and conducting ourselves in a holy manner. 1 Peter 2:12 encourages: “Maintain your conduct fine among the nations, so that... they may glorify God.”

4. What can damage Jehovah’s reputation?

When someone who claims to serve Jehovah lives hypocritically, it brings reproach. Romans 2:24 warns: “The name of God is being blasphemed among the nations because of you.”

5. Why is Jehovah’s name so important?

Jehovah’s name represents who He is — His reputation and purpose. Psalm 83:18 says: “You, whose name is Jehovah, you alone are the Most High.” His name is key to true worship.

6. How does our behavior reflect on Jehovah’s name?

Our conduct can either glorify or disgrace God’s name. Titus 2:10 says we should “adorn the teaching of our Savior, God,” meaning our lives should make God’s standards appealing.

7. How can young ones give glory to Jehovah?

Young ones give glory to Jehovah by showing respect for His standards, even when under peer pressure. Proverbs 27:11 says: “Be wise, my son, and make my heart rejoice, so that I can make a reply to him who taunts me.”

8. What role does prayer play in glorifying Jehovah?

Through prayer, we acknowledge Jehovah’s position and show reliance on Him. Philippians 4:6 says: “In everything by prayer... let your petitions be made known to God.”

9. Why should we be determined to avoid bringing reproach on Jehovah’s name?

Because we represent Him, our actions impact how others view Jehovah. 2 Corinthians 6:3 states: “We are giving no cause for stumbling, so that our ministry might not be criticized.”

10. What blessings come from glorifying Jehovah?

Those who glorify Jehovah enjoy His approval, peace of mind, and hope for everlasting life. John 12:26 promises: “If anyone ministers to me, the Father will honor him.”
