Give Glory to Jehovah
Expand Study Article

Theme Scripture:

“Give Jehovah the glory due his name.” — Psalm 96:8

FOCUS:

We will consider reasons to glorify Jehovah and practical ways to do so.

1. Many today focus on what?

Many people are self-absorbed, seeking attention through things like social media and personal accomplishments, rather than giving glory to Jehovah.

2. How did Jehovah reveal his glory at Mount Sinai?

Jehovah made a powerful display of His glory through a trembling mountain, lightning, thunder, and the sound of a horn.

Exodus 19:16-18, 24:17; Psalm 68:8

3. What does it mean to give Jehovah glory?

We glorify Jehovah by praising His qualities, speaking of His deeds, and crediting Him for our accomplishments.

Isaiah 26:12; 1 Chronicles 29:11, 20

4. How did Jesus give glory to Jehovah?

Jesus glorified Jehovah by acknowledging Him as the Source of his power and by treating others with compassion.

Mark 5:18–20; Luke 13:10–13

5. What reasons do we have to respect Jehovah?

He is almighty, wise, loyal, and always fulfills His promises.

Psalm 96:4–7; Revelation 4:11; Revelation 15:4; Joshua 23:14; Jeremiah 10:6–7

6. Why do we love Jehovah?

Because He is merciful, empathetic, and humble.

Psalm 103:13; Isaiah 49:15; Zechariah 2:8; Psalm 25:14; Acts 17:27; Psalm 113:6–7

7. What special opportunity do we have?

We can expose Satan’s lies and help others know the true God.

2 Corinthians 4:4; Isaiah 43:10

8. What is one way we can glorify Jehovah?

By speaking about Him and sharing the good news.

Psalm 96:1–3

9–10. What do you learn from Angelena’s experience?

Even under pressure, we can glorify Jehovah by defending His name respectfully and with courage.

1 Peter 3:15

11. How have true worshippers followed Psalm 96:8?

They honor Jehovah with their material things.

Psalm 96:8; Proverbs 3:9; 1 Chronicles 29:3–9; Luke 8:1–3; Acts 11:27–29

12. How can our donations bring glory to Jehovah?

They support relief work, and when others see Jehovah’s care, they are drawn to Him.

13. How can our conduct bring glory to Jehovah?

By living clean lives and reflecting Jehovah’s qualities.

Psalm 96:9; Psalm 24:3–4; 1 Peter 1:15–16; Colossians 3:9–10

14. What do you learn from Jack’s experience?

Even deeply flawed people can change, showing the power of Jehovah’s spirit and glorifying His name.

1 Peter 2:12

15. How will Jehovah glorify his name in the near future?

He will execute judgment on false religion and Satan’s system, sanctifying His name completely.

Psalm 96:10–13; Revelation 17:5, 16; 19:1–2; Mark 8:38; 2 Thessalonians 1:6–10; Revelation 20:7–10; Habakkuk 2:14

16. What are you determined to do?

Continue giving Jehovah the glory due His name in everything — now and into the future.

Psalm 96:8

HOW WOULD YOU ANSWER?

1. What does it mean to give Jehovah glory?
Acknowledge Jehovah’s greatness, speak boldly about Him, and credit Him for blessings. (Isaiah 26:12; 1 Chron. 29:11; Psalm 96:3)

2. What motivates us to give Jehovah glory?
Love and reverence for Jehovah and the desire to share the truth. (Jer. 10:6–7; Rev. 4:11; Psalm 103:13; Zech. 2:8; Isa. 49:15)

3. How can we give Jehovah the glory he deserves?
By our words, our conduct, our generosity, and our loyalty. (Psalm 96:1–3; Prov. 3:9; 1 Peter 1:16; 2 Thess. 1:6–10)
