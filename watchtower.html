Give Glory to Jehovah

Theme Scripture:
“Give Jehovah the glory due his name.” — Psalm 96:8

FOCUS:
We will consider reasons to glorify Jehovah and practical ways to do so.

1. Many today focus on what?

Many people are self-absorbed, seeking attention through things like social media and personal accomplishments, rather than giving glory to Jehovah.

2. How did Jehovah reveal his glory at Mount Sinai?

Jehovah made a powerful display of His glory through a trembling mountain, lightning, thunder, and the sound of a horn.
Exodus 19:16-18, 24:17; Psalm 68:8

Husbands, Honor Your Wife

Theme Scripture:
“You husbands, . . . assign them honor.” — 1 Peter 3:7

FOCUS:
How a husband can honor his wife in word and action.

1. What is one reason why Jehovah created the marriage arrangement?

Jehovah is “the happy God,” and He wants us to be happy too. One of His gifts is marriage, which brings joy when maintained with love and respect.
1 Tim. 1:11; Jas. 1:17; Prov. 5:18

2. What is the state of many marriages today?

Many couples forget their wedding vows. Abuse and pornography are common, damaging relationships and happiness.

3. What factors can contribute to a husband’s abusive behavior?

Some are raised by violent fathers, influenced by harmful cultures, or corrupted by pornography. COVID-19 stress worsened these issues.

4. What do Christian husbands need to guard against, and why?

They must avoid worldly thinking. Wrong thoughts can lead to wrong actions. Paul warned Christians not to conform to the world.
Rom. 12:1, 2

5. According to 1 Peter 3:7, how should a husband treat his wife?

With honor and kindness, reflecting genuine respect. Failing to do so harms his relationship with Jehovah.
1 Peter 3:7

FOUR WAYS TO BE A RESPECTFUL HUSBAND
1. Use polite speech, such as “please” and “thank you.”
2. Praise your wife and avoid exposing her faults.
3. Be quick to listen, slow to speak.
4. Speak mildly, even during intense conversations.
