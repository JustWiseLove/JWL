Cain & Abel (Genesis 4) - Reincarnation/Faith

Genesis 4:1-12 recounts Cain and Abel, Adam’s sons, offering sacrifices to Jehovah. Abel, a shepherd, gave his flock’s firstlings with heartfelt faith, pleasing Jehovah (Genesis 4:4). Faith
Abel’s faith was active, shown in his sacrifice. Faith without works is dead (James 2:26). We build faith through obedience and sharing Jehovah’s truth. Let’s share a scripture this week, reflecting Abel’s devotion. Discuss: How can we show Abel’s faith in our worship? This lesson strengthens trust in Jehovah’s justice and resurrection hope, guiding our family to live faithfully for His Kingdom. (248 words)
