Finding the True Path: A Bible Study Journey

Life can lead us down confusing paths, filled with ideas that seem true but leave us lost. This 88-lesson Bible study reveals Jehovah’s loving guidance through His Word, helping you build a relationship with Him and trust in the Bible’s truth. The study is divided into four sections: Building a Relationship with Jehovah (1–22), Trusting the Bible as God’s Word (23–44), Examining Ourselves with God’s Word (45–66), and Worship That God Approves (67–88). Each lesson includes an Illustration (I), Discussion (D) with a question and scripture, Explanation (E), and Application (A).

Image Suggestion: Source a grayscale image of a GPS device showing a wrong route from Unsplash or Pexels.

D: How can we find the right path to God? “This means everlasting life, their coming to know you, the only true God, and the one whom you sent, Jesus Christ.” (John 17:3)
